Custom directives demo

Imagine a CMS (Content management system) where the contents of the page is read from a server. This means that every page could be completely dynamic. It also means that it should be maintained by an administrator.

To make that happen, we're going to allow a user to double-click on any elment that is marked with *content-editable and watch it become, well, editable.
